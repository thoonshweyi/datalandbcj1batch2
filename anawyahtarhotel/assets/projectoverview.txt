About Golden Rose Guest House
Golden Rose Guest House provides a 24-hour reception, as well as luggage storage and free Wi-Fi. Additionally, the multilingual staff are on hand to provide local information.

The guest house features 19 air conditioned rooms filled with all the necessities to ensure a comfortable stay. Guests can relax in the homely seating area.

Golden Rose Guest House is within walking distance of Bagan. It is also perfectly located in close proximity to a range of eateries.
